You head down the hall to storage closet and decide to take a quick stock of your inventory. Opening your stash, you find a well kept assortment of medieval maces and battle axes; a fitting weapon for such a hard-hitting brute such as yourself. One cannot even begin to comprehend the joy you would feel from carving into any of those time crazy bastards. However, since one of your targets is Sawbuck, you’re afraid that you’ll have to hold from using any weapons to keep from activating his time jumping ability. To make matters easier, you at least hope that your other target Cans is not with him once you leave to check the south wing of the joint. Yet now that you’re here thinking about it, you still haven’t gotten a good look at Snowman, and the sight of her banged up on the floor would put you in a good mood. As such, you consider going back to the first room.
