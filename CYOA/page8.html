You question him on what he could possibly know. He responds with a riddle on the events of the past couple of hours. “She’s no man, but thinks like one; getting into your head to poke some fun; a crime attempted behind a door; but all is fair in love war.” While you stand there trying to glean anything from that, he sneaks off with a final chuckle echoing among the halls. Too preoccupied to pursue him, you come to the realization that you were the one to shoot her over a flash of rage. It must be, you think, you're the only one in the gang who's been able to catch her and all of the Felt members cease fire when she's around. But wait, what did Clover mean when he talked about love and war? It appears you’ve solved this case and have a bit of buried emotions to unearth.
