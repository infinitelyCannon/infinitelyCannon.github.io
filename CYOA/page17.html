You burst into room with a bucket of energy and not much else, certainly not a plan. Itchy, being quicker than a wink via his time alteration, slips out of the room fast enough to have one wonder if he was there in the first place. This ambiguous one excludes you, however, as you focus in strictly on Doze who is slow to reaction. In one fell swoop you have the crook apprehended in front of you. Proud of your daring accomplishment, you proceed to interrogate him for any knowledge on Snowman’s condition. Silence, just as you expected out of him. He appears to have already activated his powers over time; the ability to slow the flow of time, but only onto himself. Awaiting an answer, you . . . . . . apparently keep waiting for a long time as Doze’s lips ever so slightly form the first syllable to what you hope is the answer you’re looking for.



~ End
