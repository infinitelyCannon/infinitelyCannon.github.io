You do your best to sneak up to Clover unnoticed. The dark cover of night works well to hide you and your black suit. With your pistol in hand you get within one foot of his head before he notices you. By then it's too late for him. You take aim at the poor chump’s head and pull the trigger. His shocked gasp turns into a mocking snicker as your gun clicks in your hand. It seems you appear to have ran out of bullets; lucky guy, that Clover. “Hoo hoo, as quick to the draw as ever aren’t we,” he says in a sudden taunt. Angered and confused, you quickly think between finding a knife to ace the smug bastard and asking him what he means by that.
