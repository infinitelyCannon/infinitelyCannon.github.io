With the promise of a mighty good brawl in your reach, you charge towards Cans and round up your fist for a suckerpunch on his leg. As you rush past Sawbuck, he fumbles with his gun in shock and tries to hurry with his next round of bullets. Not being able to get a solid aim at you, he instead calls out to Cans to try to warn him. You quicken your pace and see that he remains still. He must not have heard him you figure. You get within a foot of Cans, but before you can even lay a dent on him he snaps around and clocks you in the face; hard enough to send you back in time few minutes prior.
