The clock ticks forward in a slow recursive stroll towards the stroke of midnight. The dim glow of the moonlight does very little to illuminate the scene of the crime that took place; and even less to soften the stiffened air. The only other light source is from a still lit cigarette on the ground. It’s vintage style holder clutched by the hands of the Felt’s eighth member, Snowman, within an inch of her life. Even as she lays unconscious she still clings to her most cherished possession. Four men stand timorously over the body wondering the same thing: who could have done this? Was it an accidental case of friendly fire from one of the Felt members’ time shenanigans? Or was this an attempted outing from the Felt’s rival gang: the Midnight Crew. ♠Spades Slick, ♦Diamonds Droog, ♣Clubs Deuce, and ♥Hearts Boxcars all stand ready to see to this situation.
